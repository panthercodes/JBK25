Jake Patterson is our Founder’s son and is 31 years old. Jake has a bachelor’s degree in Arts and Entertainment Media Management from Columbia College in Chicago. Jake works with JBK Search as Director Outreach. Jake has a passion for promoting local artists at festivals and music venues. He lives in Chicago with his partner and their Shiba Inu, Lennon.

Jake was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes at the age of 17. Like his sister with her CF, Jake never allowed diabetes to define him. He quickly learned how to prick his finger to test for sugars, manage highs and low, inject insulin, and manage his diet. Jake followed clinical trials and the advancements of the artificial pancreas and insulin pump. He was one of the first in line to start using this amazing technology. Thank you to Roche, Dexcom, and the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.

He strives to make a difference by providing financial support, time and energy to the CF Foundation and JDRF. In 2019, he participated in the One Dream Gala for JDRF in Chicago where a record-breaking $7.1 million was raised for T1D research. In 2018 and 2019, Jake was honored as an Ambassador to raise money and awareness at the Purple Tie Ball, a high-end fundraising event for the CF Foundation.
