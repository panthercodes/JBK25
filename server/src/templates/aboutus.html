The Recruitment of Possibility

JBK Search is a global recruiting firm committed to connecting top talent with passion-driven organizations in the biotechnology, biopharma, molecular diagnostics, medical devices & software, labratory services and nonprofit industries.

We are experienced recruiters. We are trusted advisors. Our clients seek us out because of our industry experience and our straightforward, no-nonsense approach to recruiting

We ask many questions during the initial assessment.

We create a profile of the candidate based on the skills needed and the personality desired.

We assemble an exhaustive list of candidates from our database of over 12,000 candidates, our extensive network, and our state-of-the-art search tools.

We conduct an initial interview and assessment, followed up by our Founder & CEO’s lengthy behavioral-based interview.

We present the candidate to the leadership team.

We coach the client on areas to dig in and the candidate’s hot buttons.

We cover the counteroffer (early & often).

We discuss any concerns or needs with the candidate’s significant others or family if needed concerning issues such as relocation.

We negotiate offers.

We help with onboarding if asked.

We stay in touch with the candidate and client after the placement to address any concerns.

AI RECRUITMENT AND THE AUTOMATION ERA

Personal connections are at the heart of JBK Search, where we focus on understanding each candidate’s unique strengths and aligning them with the perfect opportunities.

JBK Search remains dedicated to human connections, offering individualized attention and thoughtful communication to both clients and candidates at every step.
